Brandon Walsh graduated in 2009 from the University of Virginia with a B.A. in English Literature and Music. In 2011, he received an M.A. in English from UVA, where he is currently working towards his doctorate. He has work published or forthcoming in Conradiana, The International Trumpet Guild Journal, and James Joyce Quarterly. He is currently a Praxis Fellow in the Scholars' Lab.

His interests include twentieth-century literature, Anglophone modernism, music and musicology, sound studies, the history of the novel, narrative theory, and James Joyce.

This website contains information about his work and research. For more information, please contact him at bmw9t@virginia.edu.
